About me

Since 2023, Ali Mohamed Ali is a Ph.D. student majoring in control systems applied to Robotics and Aerospace applications. He is a teaching and research assistant in the Robotics, Navigation and Control Systems Laboratory lab (RNCSL) at Carleton University.


Academic Degrees

M.Eng. & B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering Department of Mechanical Engineering, Alexandria University, Alexandria, Egypt, 2019.
